THE WORLD'S LARGEST FORUM FOR WOMEN LEADERS

WE Convention is the world's largest and most influential women’s forum, bringing together global icons, industry leaders, and rising changemakers to shape the future. With over 300 million media mentions, WE Convention is one of the most prominent voices in the space of women’s leadership, entrepreneurship, and success.

How do you reach exceptional career success, achieve financial independence, and still lead a fulfilling life beyond work? On the WE Convention stage, we bring the world’s most powerful women to share their journeys, insights, and strategies — so you can go all in on your ambitions without compromise. This is more than a conference. This is a movement.
